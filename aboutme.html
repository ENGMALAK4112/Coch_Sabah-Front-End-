About

My name is Sabah Derhem, and I am a certified life coach and trainer. I am committed to helping individuals achieve their personal and spiritual growth by providing a non-judgmental and supportive environment. I believe in working holistically with clients to identify and address any limiting beliefs, behavior patterns, and negative self-talk to facilitate growth and transformation. My approach is tailored to each client's unique needs and preferences, and I strive to empower individuals to take control of their lives and achieve their full potential. My mission as a life coach is to help you become the best version of yourself. Through one-on-one coaching sessions, we will identify areas of your life where you may feel stuck, and work together to create a personalized roadmap to success.
