My Book

Soulful Dance Into the Tune of Life

Soulful Dance Into the Tune of Life is a reflective self-help book that guides you to embrace your soul's journey with faith, grace, and trust in divine timing. Blending spiritual insight with personal depth, Sabah Derhem offers a new way of understanding fate — not as something fixed, but as a loyal companion inviting us into greater awareness, healing, and conscious choice.

This book is for anyone who has ever asked "Why am I here?" or "What is the meaning behind my life's challenges?" It's a gentle invitation to see life as a sacred dance — one where every step, even the painful ones, leads us closer to truth, joy, and alignment.
